Elphant ear

Ingrediants

  1. Flour
  2. Salt
  3. Baking Powder
  4. warm water
  5. Oil- Peanut, vegetable or canola oil work best for frying.
  6. Sugar
  7. Cinnamon

Prep

  1. Add the flour, salt and baking soda. Stir in the warm water until a dough forms. Then let the dough sit for 5 minutes.
  2. Roll the dough out slightly into thin sheets and cut it into 6 pieces. Next, take each of those pieces and roll into balls of doug
  3. Then flatten into Â½ inch thick circles.
  4. Place the dough circles in the hot oil and fry until the puff pastry is golden brown. Remove from heat and place on a plate with paper towels.
  5. Mix together the cinnamon and sugar in a small bowl.
  6. Sprinkle the elephant ears with cinnamon and sugar mixture while warm.
